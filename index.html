How it works

It's a real conversation. Just not one you have to have.

Sentrel sounds like a friendly office manager who knows your business. It asks the right questions, books the job, and gets you the details in plain English.

1

Customer calls your shop number.

We forward your line — keep your number, keep your reputation. After two rings, Sentrel picks up.

2

It asks the right questions.

Address. What's broken. How urgent. If it's a leak, ask for a photo by text. If it's an emergency, route straight to your cell.

3

You get a text with everything you need.

Customer name, address, problem, photos, and a confirmed time slot already on the calendar. Show up, do the work.
